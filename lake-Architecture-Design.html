EXPLORE LAKE APO

Architecture & Design of Lake Apo

Lake Apo features a natural crater design surrounded by gently sloping hills and dense vegetation. The area is enhanced with simple, eco-friendly structures such as bamboo rafts, viewing decks, and cottages that blend with the environment. Pathways and resting areas are designed to preserve the lake’s natural beauty while providing visitors with safe and relaxing access to scenic spots.
