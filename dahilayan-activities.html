EXPLORE DAHILAYAN ADVENTURE PARK
  • Zipline
    Experience one of the park’s most famous attractions—the 840-meter dual zipline, known as one of the longest in Asia. Riders are launched from high elevation and “fly” across the forest at speeds of 60–90 kph, offering breathtaking views of the mountains and lush greenery.
  • ATV Ride
    The ATV (All-Terrain Vehicle) ride lets visitors explore rugged trails and natural surroundings. It is perfect for adventure lovers who want an off-road experience while enjoying the scenic landscape of Bukidnon.
  • Dropzone
    The Dropzone is a 120-foot freefall ride that swings riders like a giant pendulum over a man-made lake. It gives an intense adrenaline rush as you drop and swoop close to the water.
