EXPLORE DAHILAYAN ADVENTURE PARK
  • Dahilayan Adventure Park is a top tourist destination in Bukidnon, known for its thrilling outdoor activities and cool mountain climate.
  • Located at the foot of the Mount Kitanglad Range, it offers a refreshing escape surrounded by pine trees and scenic views.
  • The park is famous for its extreme rides, including Asia’s longest dual zipline, where visitors can experience high-speed adventure.
  • It also features attractions like Dropzone, Mountain Coaster, and Rollerzip, making it perfect for both thrill-seekers and families.
  • With its mix of adventure and nature, Dahilayan Adventure Park is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Bukidnon.
