🍽️ Food & Dining Options Around Dahilayan Adventure Park

Inside/Very Close to the Park

  • Jessie's Diner – Casual diner near rides (salisbury steak and other comfort foods mentioned by visitors). Tripadvisor
  • Forest Cafe – Coffee shop and light snacks (some visitors have noted food here, though reviews vary). Tripadvisor
  • Food stalls / Cafés inside the park’s common area – Local food stalls and a café/canteen where you can grab snacks, drinks, and quick meals. OSMIVA

Nearby Restaurants & Eateries

  • Dahilayan Pizza Shack – Highly rated pizza place great for group meals.
  • Nang Ka Tasa – Local restaurant with affordable meals and snacks.
  • Dahilayan Food Hub – Family restaurant with varied menu (great for lunch/dinner).
  • Allegra's Cafe and Resto Bar – Resto bar with local dishes and drinks.
  • Antonios Café – Coffee shop + light bites, good for coffee breaks.
  • SJ Diner – Classic diner spot with meals at moderate prices.
  • Ricardo's by Cucina Higala – Full-service restaurant with Filipino and international dishes. Evendo

🍔 Typical Food You Can Expect

  • Burgers 🍔, pizza 🍕, fries 🍟, and hotdog snacks
  • Grilled/barbecue items
  • Filipino comfort meals (rice dishes, grilled meats)
  • Salads and pasta
  • Coffee, iced tea, juice, and desserts 🧁
