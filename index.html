KAAMULAN FESTIVAL

March 21 – April 23

Experience the vibrant culture of Bukidnon! Celebrity Night on April 13 featuring TJ Monterde & KZ Tandingan. Celebrate the rich traditions of Bukidnon's seven indigenous groups — Higaonon, Manobo, Matigsalug, Talaandig, Tigwahanon, and Umayamnon. Don't miss the colorful festivities, cultural performances, and a showcase of indigenous heritage!
